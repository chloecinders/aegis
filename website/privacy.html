Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the public instance of the Ouroboros Discord bot collects, uses, and protects your data. Transparency is our core philosophy, ensuring that server administrators and their members understand exactly what information is processed to enable our moderation features.

2. Data We Collect

To function effectively as a moderation bot, Ouroboros inherently must read and occasionally store specific data from your Discord server:

  • Discord User Details: User IDs, usernames, and avatar hashes.
  • Discord Server Details: Guild IDs, channel IDs, and role IDs necessary for permissions and configuration.
  • Message Content: The Bot relies on the Message Content Intent to read commands, process replies, and handle automated moderation rules. Message contents are primarily kept in an ephemeral, memory-only dynamic cache.
  • Audit Log Data: Data retrieved from Discord's Audit logs to enrich moderation context (e.g., discovering who deleted a message).
  • Moderation Records: Specific historical logs of user infractions (warns, kicks, bans) invoked by your moderation team.

3. How We Use the Data

Any data collected is strictly utilized to provide and fulfill the core moderation functionality of Ouroboros. Specifically:

  • Temporary Caching: Message content is cached dynamically to enable rapid contextual retrieval, sniping deleted messages, and resolving reply targets without hitting Discord API rate limits.
  • Persistent Storage: Moderation records, configured server prefixes, mapping permissions, and explicit moderation log context are stored securely in a PostgreSQL database to ensure persistent tracking of administrative actions.

We do not sell, distribute, or share your data with third parties. There is absolutely no external advertising tracking, user profiling, or monetization of the information passing through the bot.

4. Data Retention

Given the bot's nature to act as a historical moderation audit log, certain records (such as specific infractions, who administered them, and contextual data surrounding those infractions) are stored indefinitely alongside your server configurations. Ephemeral message caches are frequently purged from memory based on channel activity and time constraints to minimize memory consumption.

5. Your Control and Data Deletion Rights

As a server administrator, you retain the right to manage your server's data. If you choose to remove Ouroboros from your server, you may request a complete and permanent wipe of all associated server configurations and moderation logs by contacting the developer team via our official GitHub repository.

Individual Discord users wishing to inquire about specific personal data tied to moderation logs should directly contact the administrators of the respective Discord server where the infractions occurred, as they retain administrative jurisdiction over their server's moderation history.

6. Self-Hosted Instances

Because Ouroboros is open-source, anyone can host their own instance. This Privacy Policy strictly applies to the official, primary public instance run by the core maintainers. If your server is utilizing a self-hosted instance of Ouroboros by a third party, data collection is handled securely on their hardware, and you must review their specific privacy guidelines.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please open an issue or reach out through our official GitHub repository at github.com/chloecinders/ouroboros.
